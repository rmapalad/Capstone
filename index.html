Frequently Asked Questions

What types of units are available?

We offer a variety of units including studio apartments, one-bedroom, two-bedroom, and three-bedroom units. Each unit is designed with modern amenities and high-quality finishes. Commercial spaces are also available in various sizes to suit your business needs.

What amenities are included?

Our property features a wide range of amenities including 24/7 security, covered parking, swimming pool, fitness center, function rooms, and landscaped gardens. Each unit comes with modern appliances and high-speed internet connectivity.

How can I schedule a viewing?

You can schedule a viewing by contacting our sales team through the contact form on our website, calling our office, or visiting our showroom. We offer both in-person and virtual tours to accommodate your preferences.

What are the payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms including cash, bank financing, and in-house financing options. Down payment requirements and monthly installments vary depending on the unit type and payment scheme chosen.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we are pet-friendly! We allow pets with certain restrictions and guidelines to ensure the comfort and safety of all residents. Please contact our management office for specific pet policies and requirements.

What are the maintenance fees?

Maintenance fees cover common area maintenance, security services, and use of amenities. The fee is calculated based on the unit size and is payable monthly. Please contact our sales team for specific rates.
